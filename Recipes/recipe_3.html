Paneer Tikka Masala

Description

Paneer tikka masala is a popular Indian dish consisting of a spicy and richly flavored Indian curry made with paneer and tikka masala. It is often served with rice and vegetables.

Ingredients

    For Paneer Tikka:

  • 250 grams paneer, cubed
  • 1/2 cup thick yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • Salt to taste

    • For the Gravy:

  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup water
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro leaves for garnish

Steps

Prepare Paneer Tikka:

  1. Marinate Paneer:

    • In a bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, cumin powder, coriander powder, lemon juice, and salt.
    • Add paneer cubes and coat them well with the marinade. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.

  2. Cook Paneer:

    • Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C) and place marinated paneer cubes on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Drizzle with oil.
    • Bake for 10-15 minutes until slightly charred. Alternatively, you can grill or pan-fry the paneer cubes.

Prepare the Gravy:

  1. Cook Onions:

    • Heat oil and butter in a pan over medium heat. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.

  2. Add Ginger-Garlic and Spices:

    • Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for another minute.
    • Add turmeric, red chili powder, cumin, and coriander powder. Cook for a minute until fragrant.

  3. Add Tomato Puree:

    • Add tomato puree and cook until the oil separates from the masala.

  4. Simmer with Cream and Water:

    • Add heavy cream, garam masala, salt, and water. Mix well and simmer for 5-7 minutes until the gravy thickens.

Combine and Serve:

  1. Add Paneer Tikka:

    • Add the cooked paneer tikka to the gravy. Simmer for another 5 minutes to let the flavors meld together.

  2. Garnish and Serve:

    • Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves.
    • Serve hot with naan, roti, or rice.

Enjoy
