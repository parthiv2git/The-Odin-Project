Ramen Noodle Soup

Description

Ramen noodle soup is a flavorful and comforting Japanese dish consisting of wheat noodles served in a rich broth, often accompanied by ingredients such as sliced pork, soft-boiled eggs, seaweed, and various vegetables. This dish is known for its delicious and complex flavors, making it a favorite comfort food worldwide.

Ingredients

    Broth:

  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 4 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon miso paste
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced/li>
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil

    • Toppings:

  • 2 packs ramen noodles
  • 2 soft-boiled eggs, halved
  • 8 oz cooked sliced chicken
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup baby spinach
  • 1/2 cup corn kernels
  • 1 sheet seaweed, cut into strips
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Prepare the Broth:

    • In a large pot, heat sesame oil over medium heat. Add garlic, ginger, and chopped green onions. Saut√© for about 2 minutes until fragrant.
    • Add chicken or vegetable broth, water, soy sauce, and miso paste. Stir well to combine.
    • Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 15-20 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.

  2. Cook the Ramen Noodles:

    • While the broth is simmering, cook the ramen noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.

  3. Prepare the Toppings:

    • Slice cooked pork or chicken.
    • Soft-boil the eggs (boil for 6-7 minutes, then place in ice water and peel).
    • Slice mushrooms and prepare other vegetables and toppings as desired.

  4. Assemble the Ramen:

    • Divide the cooked ramen noodles into bowls.
    • Pour the hot broth over the noodles.
    • Arrange the toppings: sliced pork or chicken, soft-boiled egg halves, mushrooms, spinach, corn, seaweed strips, and green onions.
    • Sprinkle sesame seeds on top.

  5. Serve:

    • Serve immediately while hot.

Enjoy
